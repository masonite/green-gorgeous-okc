### **Step 2: Manual Process (30 minutes per video)**

#### **A. Research (5 minutes)**
1. Google the business name + "Oklahoma City"
2. Find their actual website (if any)
3. Take 3-4 screenshots:
   - Homepage
   - Services page
   - Contact page
   - Mobile view (use Chrome DevTools)

#### **B. Analysis (10 minutes)**
Use this checklist:
- [ ] **Mobile friendly?** Test on phone simulator
- [ ] **Contact info visible?** Phone number above the fold?
- [ ] **Services clear?** Can you tell what they do in 3 seconds?
- [ ] **Page speed?** Use Google PageSpeed Insights
- [ ] **Design modern?** Looks professional or outdated?

#### **C. Script (5 minutes)**
Follow this template:
```
"Hi [Business Name], I was reviewing your website and noticed [1-2 specific issues]. 

For example, [specific example from their site]. 

This matters because [explain impact - e.g., '85% of customers search on mobile first'].

The good news is, for $500 we can have this fixed in 24 hours. You'd get [benefit 1, benefit 2, benefit 3].

Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call this week?"
```

#### **D. Recording (10 minutes)**
1. **Screen recording software:**
   - Mac: QuickTime Player (free)
   - Windows: Xbox Game Bar (Win+G)
   - Chrome: Loom extension (free)
   
2. **Record:**
   - Show their website (30 seconds)
   - Point out 2-3 issues with mouse cursor
   - Show our template comparison (30 seconds)
   - End with call-to-action (15 seconds)

3. **Add voiceover:**
   - Record audio separately
   - Or use text-to-speech: https://elevenlabs.io/text-to-speech

### **Step 3: Delivery**
1. **Upload to private hosting:**
