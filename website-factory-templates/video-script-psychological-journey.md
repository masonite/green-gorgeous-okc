# Website Factory - Video Script (Psychological Journey)

## **Video Length:** 90 seconds
## **Style:** Emotional storytelling, not technical demo
## **Goal:** Move viewers through pain → desire → solution → action

---

## **SCENE 1: THE PROBLEM (0-20s)**
**Visual:** Contractor looking frustrated, phone not ringing, watching competitors get jobs
**Music:** Slightly tense, minor key
**Voiceover:**
> "Every day, lawn care businesses miss opportunities. Customers search online... but can't find them. Phone doesn't ring. Jobs go to competitors. It's frustrating. It's costing them money."

**On-screen text:** "3-5 missed jobs every month"

---

## **SCENE 2: PAIN AMPLIFICATION (20-35s)**
**Visual:** Calendar flipping, dollar signs disappearing, contractor looking at empty schedule
**Music:** Building tension
**Voiceover:**
> "The math is simple. Each missed job: $75. Every month: $300 lost. Every year: $3,600 gone. That's real money leaving their business."

**On-screen text:** "$3,600 lost every year"

---

## **SCENE 3: DESIRE CREATION (35-50s)**
**Visual:** Contractor imagining success - phone ringing, calendar filling, smiling
**Music:** Shifts to hopeful, major key
**Voiceover:**
> "Now imagine the opposite. Waking up to new leads. Customers finding you easily. Booking jobs without phone tag. Looking as professional as the big companies."

**On-screen text:** "Imagine: 5 new jobs every week"

---

## **SCENE 4: SOCIAL PROOF (50-65s)**
**Visual:** Quick cuts of happy contractors, before/after website shots, testimonials
**Music:** Confident, upbeat
**Voiceover:**
> "This isn't a dream. Mike's Lawn Care did it. Green Thumb Services did it. 12 lawn care businesses in your city fixed this problem. They got websites. They got results."

**On-screen text:** "12 local businesses transformed"

---

## **SCENE 5: SOLUTION REVEAL (65-75s)**
**Visual:** Beautiful website templates appear (brief flashes, not detailed)
**Music:** Solution-oriented, clear
**Voiceover:**
> "Website Factory. Professional websites for lawn care businesses. Live in 24 hours. One flat fee: $500. No monthly costs. You own everything."

**On-screen text:** "24 hours • $500 • No monthly fees"

---

## **SCENE 6: SIMPLICITY (75-82s)**
**Visual:** Simple 4-step animation: 1) Choose design, 2) Fill form, 3) We build, 4) Go live
**Music:** Simple, clean
**Voiceover:**
> "The process is simple. Choose a design. Give us your info. We build in 24 hours. You get leads."

**On-screen text:** "Simple 4-step process"

---

## **SCENE 7: SCARCITY & URGENCY (82-87s)**
**Visual:** Counter showing "1 spot left", clock ticking
**Music:** Urgent but positive
**Voiceover:**
> "We only take 10 clients per month. Right now: one spot left. 24-hour delivery guarantee."

**On-screen text:** "1 spot left • 24-hour guarantee"

---

## **SCENE 8: CALL TO ACTION (87-90s)**
**Visual:** Contractor smiling, website live, getting calls
**Music:** Triumphant, resolved
**Voiceover:**
> "Stop losing jobs. Start getting leads. Claim your spot now at [WebsiteURL]."

**On-screen text:** "[WebsiteURL] • Claim your spot"

---

## **Video Production Notes:**

### **What to SHOW:**
1. **Emotions** - Frustration → Hope → Confidence → Relief
2. **Transformation** - Before (problem) → After (solution)
3. **Social proof** - Real businesses, real results
4. **Simplicity** - Easy process, not complex tech
5. **Urgency** - Limited availability

### **What to HIDE (Keep Magic):**
1. **No live editing** - Don't show the automation
2. **No technical details** - Don't explain how it works
3. **No template switching** - Just show beautiful results
4. **No backend** - Keep the "factory" mysterious

### **Visual Style:**
- **Real footage** of contractors (stock or filmed)
- **Clean animations** for numbers/process
- **Professional color grading**
- **Quick cuts** to maintain pace
- **Emotional close-ups** on faces

### **Voiceover Tone:**
- **Empathetic** in problem section
- **Hopeful** in desire section  
- **Confident** in solution section
- **Urgent** in CTA section

### **Music Journey:**
1. **0-20s:** Tense, minor (problem)
2. **20-35s:** Building tension (pain)
3. **35-50s:** Hopeful shift (desire)
4. **50-65s:** Confident, upbeat (proof)
5. **65-82s:** Clear, solution-oriented (solution)
6. **82-87s:** Urgent but positive (scarcity)
7. **87-90s:** Triumphant, resolved (CTA)
